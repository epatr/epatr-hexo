Music

I studied classical guitar under a student of Segovia at the Stetson University Community School of Music. There, I performed in their guitar ensemble from 1996-1999. I've been playing guitar and bass in bands for the past 15 years, performing all over the southeast USA. I strongly believe in making my music accessible for free to everyone, so feel free to download these albums and enjoy!
